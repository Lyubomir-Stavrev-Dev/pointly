## Launch-day playbook
1. **Reply to every single comment, fast.** Engagement drives ranking more than raw upvotes.
2. Share the PH link on your channels with "we're live today" — X, LinkedIn, relevant Discords/Slacks. **Never ask for upvotes directly** (against PH rules; can get you delisted). "Check out my launch, feedback welcome" is fine.
3. r/macapps post the same day (see below) — that community genuinely loves new Mac utilities.
4. Add the PH badge to trypointly.com once live (they give you an embed snippet on your post page). Ping me — I'll add it to the hero.
5. Watch for feature requests in comments — replying "shipped!" 48h later is the best marketing that exists.
