Product Research

What makes a demo actually land

Internal memo · 6 min read

We reviewed forty recorded product demos from the last two quarters and scored them against the deals they produced. Slide quality mattered less than we expected, and talk time mattered even less.

The demos that converted best were the ones where the audience always knew exactly where to look.

Presenters who anchored attention — pointing, highlighting, dimming everything else — kept prospects engaged for twice as long as presenters who narrated over a static screen.
