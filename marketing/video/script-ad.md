# Ad scripts — 45s main + 15s cutdown

Unlike the App Store preview, ads have no Apple content rules — lead with the *problem*, be a little funny, and hook in the first 2 seconds. Both cuts are designed sound-off (captions carry the story); music still recommended.

**Deliverables per cut (assemble.sh produces all three):** 16:9 1920×1080 (YouTube, web), 1:1 1080×1080 (feeds), 9:16 1080×1920 (Reels / TikTok / Shorts).

## Placement notes
- **9:16 / 1:1:** the pipeline letterboxes the 16:9 footage on the brand-dark background — captions stay readable and nothing is cropped. If a platform's performance justifies it later, re-shoot key clips zoomed-in for native vertical.
- **Hook discipline:** platforms decide in ~1.5s whether to keep serving the ad. Clip `00-problem` must be instantly relatable — a cursor flailing on a slide is universal.
- **Music:** upbeat, 100–120 BPM, no vocals. Licensed for ads: Artlist, Epidemic Sound, or free — Uppbeat / Pixabay Music (check the ad-use license tier). Do **not** use iMovie/GarageBand loops in paid ads without checking terms.
- **CTA:** the outro card is the only CTA. On platforms with a button (Meta/TikTok), set the button to "Download" → Mac App Store link.
