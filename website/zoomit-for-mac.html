Why there's no ZoomIt on Mac

ZoomIt is part of Microsoft's Sysinternals suite and is built on Windows-only APIs, so there's no official ZoomIt for macOS and no plans for one. Mac presenters, teachers, and streamers have long needed the same thing: zoom into the screen and annotate on top of it, live. That's exactly what Pointly does — designed natively for macOS.

Everything you used ZoomIt for — on your Mac

  • Presenter Zoom — freeze the screen and magnify any detail, then pan around while zoomed in.
  • Draw & annotate live — pen, highlighter, arrows, shapes, and text directly over any app.
  • Spotlight — dim everything except the area you're presenting.
  • Laser pointer — a glowing dot with a fading trail to guide attention.
  • Countdown timer — ZoomIt's break-timer, reimagined as a clean on-screen countdown.
  • Presenter cues — show your clicks and keystrokes on screen for tutorials and streams.
