Quick start

Press ⌘⇧P to show or hide the Pointly overlay. Pick a tool from the floating toolbar (or press ⌃1–⌃9) and draw anywhere on your screen. Press ⌘⎋ to switch to Interact mode when you need to click things underneath your annotations.

Permissions Pointly asks for

Screen Recording — used only by the Cut & Move tool to capture a region of your screen so you can reposition it. Accessibility — used only so keyboard shortcuts keep working while you interact with other apps. Pointly never records, stores, or transmits anything. See the Privacy Policy.

Restore a purchase

Open the Pointly Pro screen and click Restore Purchase. Make sure you're signed into the Mac App Store with the Apple ID that made the original purchase.
