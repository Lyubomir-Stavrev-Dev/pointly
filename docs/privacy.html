Pointly Privacy Policy

Effective date: July 3, 2026
The short version: Pointly does not collect, store, or transmit any of your personal data. Everything stays on your Mac.

Data collection

Pointly collects no data whatsoever. The app contains no analytics, no tracking, no advertising SDKs, and no third-party services. We never see what you draw, what's on your screen, or how you use the app.

Screen recording permission

Pointly requests macOS Screen Recording permission solely to power the Cut & Move tool, which captures a region of your screen so you can reposition it. Captures are processed entirely in memory on your Mac and are never saved or transmitted anywhere by Pointly.

Accessibility permission

Pointly requests macOS Accessibility permission only to support global keyboard shortcuts while you interact with other apps. Pointly does not log, store, or transmit keystrokes.

Crash logs

If Pointly crashes, a diagnostic log is written locally to ~/Library/Logs/Pointly on your Mac. These logs never leave your computer unless you choose to send them to us yourself.

Purchases

Pointly Pro purchases and subscriptions are processed entirely by Apple through the Mac App Store. We do not receive or store your payment information. Apple's handling of that data is described in the Apple Privacy Policy.

Changes to this policy

If this policy ever changes, the updated version will be published at this address with a new effective date.
